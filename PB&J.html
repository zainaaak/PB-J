PB&J Recipe

Learn how make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Strawberry Jelly

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Peanut butter on one slice.
  3. pread strawberry jelly on the other slice.
  4. Put the two slices together.
